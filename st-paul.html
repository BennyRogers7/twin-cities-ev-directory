Yes, St. Paul requires an electrical permit for all Level 2 EV charger installations.

What your installer handles: All licensed electricians in our directory will pull the permit, submit required documentation, and coordinate the inspection on your behalf. This is typically included in their installation quote.

About 30-40% of homes need panel upgrades.

You likely need an upgrade if:

  • Your panel is 100 amps or less
  • Fewer than 4 empty breaker spaces
  • Already running electric heat, central AC, and other high-draw appliances

Panel upgrade cost: $1,800-$3,500 for standard 100A to 200A upgrade

Good news: Licensed electricians will perform a free load calculation to determine if your home needs an upgrade before providing a quote.
